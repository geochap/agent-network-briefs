Agent Network Briefs

We’re experimenting with a new kind of report: briefings generated by networks of simulated experts who argue, converge, diverge—and sometimes surprise us. It’s early days, but we think this collective reasoning model offers something different than solitary LLM takes or old-school analyst notes.

The system is built on some of our patented agentic tech. For now, we’re sharing sample outputs. Feedback welcome, skeptics encouraged, curious minds especially.
