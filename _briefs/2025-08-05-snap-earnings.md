## Materiality & Impact Assessment

### **Theme 1 — AI-Driven Ad Automation & SMB Engagement**

#### **Scale**
- Material to top-line growth: case studies show ROAS doubling and conversion volume up 80 % with 50 % lower CPA after Smart Bidding [[33]](#post-33); eCPM and CPC improvements of 77 % and 69 % for another SMB example [[36]](#post-36).  
- Expands advertiser base: SMBs were “the largest contributor to ad-revenue growth in Q2” [[33]](#post-33).  
- Risk side: opaque optimisation can undermine trust, especially after the auction mis-pricing that “led to sudden swings in campaign pricing and performance” [[6]](#post-6).

#### **Timing**
- Immediate impact already visible in Q2 2025 results and case studies [[33]](#post-33).  
- Mid-term (2025–26) importance grows as automation is rolled out more broadly to smart budget and auto-targeting [[33]](#post-33).

#### **Dependencies**
  - Continued SMB adoption of Smart Bidding / Smart Campaign Solutions [[33]](#post-33).  
  - No repeat of auction platform errors that disrupt pricing [[6]](#post-6).  
  - Snap providing clearer diagnostics to mitigate “risk of opaque campaign results” [[1]](#post-1).  
  - Incremental inventory from Sponsored Snaps being matched by new SMB demand [[16]](#post-16).

#### **Net Impact**
- Likely positive if trust is maintained: automation demonstrably lifts ROI and widens the advertiser funnel, supporting revenue growth and eCPM recovery.  
- Sensitivity: a single platform glitch or persistent black-box perception could stall uptake and leave new inventory under-monetised.

#### **Dependencies**

#### **Net Impact**
- Ambiguous: large upside optionality but equally large capital-at-risk.  Payoff hinges on adoption curves and external regulatory factors that Snap cannot fully control.

#### **Dependencies**

  - Cross-border data-transfer rules impacting cloud processing [[34]](#post-34).  
  - Absence (or presence) of formal regulator clearances [[25]](#post-25).

#### **Net Impact**
- Likely negative in the absence of proactive mitigation; creates headline, cost, and timing risk that could cap realisation of AR and ad-signal opportunities.
